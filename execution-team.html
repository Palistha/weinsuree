Philip Visali

Chief Execution Officer

Phil started his insurance career in 1997 with Bankers Life and Casualty as a Life and Health sales agent selling Medicare supplements to the senior market while attending the University of Florida in Gainesville. The following year, Phil was hired as a customer service representative with Nationwide Insurance in Gainesville and was instantly hooked on the P&C side of insurance. Due to the limited availability of new agency owners with Nationwide, he opened his first insurance agency with Allstate Insurance in Sarasota, Florida. He received many honors and awards for his service. In 2009, Phil founded WE Insure Florida Inc., because he found representing only one company was not in his client’s best interest, and limited his potential for growth. After only six years, WE Insure has over 70 franchised locations throughout Florida and Georgia, and is continuously being recognized as one of Northeast Florida’s largest independent agencies (2013-2015 Business Journal), Best Places to Work (2013-2015 Business Journal), Fastest Growing Private Companies (2013-2015 Business Journal) and most recently, We Insure was ranked in the top 25% of America’s Fastest-Growing Private Companies by Inc. Magazine (August, 2015 Inc. 5000 awards #1272/5000). Additionally, Phil received the “40 Under 40” award in 2013 by the Business Journal for his business model success and in 2015, Phil was also recognized by the publication as an Ultimate CEO. Phil enjoys spending time with his son Michael and you can usually find him on the golf course.
