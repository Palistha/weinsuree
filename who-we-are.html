HISTORY

Philip began his insurance career in Sarasota, Florida. After years as a captive agent, a representative of a single insurer, Philip decided to become his own boss. He yearned to break free of the restrictions that came with being a captive agent. He knew there had to be a better way to shop for and sell the insurance products we all need.

Establishing an independent agency, however, proved challenging. After years of building relationships with top insurance companies, Phil envisioned a new and better way to buy and sell insurance.
